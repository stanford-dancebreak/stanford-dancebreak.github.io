Get ready for the Stanford Viennese Ball with Stanford's fortnight of beginner
dance lessons!

<!--more-->

Every year, the [Stanford Viennese Ball](https://vienneseball.stanford.edu)
committee runs a series of daily workshops on various dance styles. Members of
various Stanford social dance clubs collaborate to teach beginner and
intermediate lessons in the many partner dance styles done at Stanford.

Austria Fortnight 2026 will start on February 13th (with the Bon Bon Ball) and
end on February 27st (with the Viennese Ball).

For up to date info, see the [Austria Fortnight
Website](https://vienneseball.stanford.edu/austria-fortnight).
